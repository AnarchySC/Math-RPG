Design document for a classic RPG that teaches arithmetic through gameplay. 10-phase development plan from engine selection to ship.

## Phases

1. Pick your engine
2. Make it playable (ugly)
3. Design your numbers
4. Build complete battle
5. Add progression
6. Create the world
7. Art pass
8. Sound and music
9. Playtest with humans
10. Polish and ship
